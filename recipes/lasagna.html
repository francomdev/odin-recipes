Lasagna

Lasagna is a traditional italian dish that consists of layers of pasta with different fillings in bewtween. The fillings usually contain meat, vegtetables, ricotta and cheese.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Noodles
  • Ricotta
  • Tomato Sauce

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Stack the ingridients in layers
  5. Cover with foil and bake

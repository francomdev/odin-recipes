Pizza

Pizza is a traditional italian dish that consists of a flat dough with toppings. The simplest pizza have tomato sauce and cheese, while it is possible to ad lots of toppings.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Yeast
  • Water
  • Olive oil
  • Muzarella
  • Tomato Sauce

Steps

  1. Mix the flour, yeast, water and olive oil to make the doug. Let it rest for an hour or until it has doubled in size.
  2. Strech the dough on a roasting pan and put some tomato sauce on tom. Afterwards add the cheese.
  3. Bake until the dough gets brownish and the cheese melts.
